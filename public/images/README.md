# Upload Images Here

Upload your service and product images here. Use the following naming convention:

## Service Images
- `service-management.jpg` - Server Management
- `server-moderation.jpg` - Server Moderation
- `custom-bots.jpg` - Custom Discord Bots (Featured)

## Product Images
- `image-generator.jpg` - Image Generator
- `video-generator.jpg` - Video Generator (Featured)
- `ai-conversation.jpg` - AI Conversation

Just drag and drop your images into this folder and they'll be automatically integrated into the website!

Recommended: 400x300px or higher resolution JPG/PNG files.
